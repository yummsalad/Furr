Rawr! # Welcome to Furr :3

Furr is currently under development. This application is being created for all those furries out there in the world trying to make new furry or scalie friends in their communities. Worry not, Furr will not only allow you to find people with your location, but also around the globe.

Simply get started by making a profile with an adorable username of your choice and your coolest furry art or personal picture you might have and you are ready to go.
